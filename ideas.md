# Bosco Restaurant — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Mediterranean Brutalism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist Mediterranean

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, honest materials translated to digital: exposed structure, bold type, unpolished edges
- High contrast between dark charcoal backgrounds and warm amber/terracotta accents
- Typographic hierarchy as the primary visual driver
- Asymmetric, tension-filled layouts that feel intentional and editorial

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black charcoal (#1a1714) evoking burnt wood and dark stone
- Primary accent: deep amber/gold (oklch 0.72 0.18 65) — the warmth of candlelight
- Secondary: terracotta rust (oklch 0.58 0.14 35) — Mediterranean clay
- Text: off-white cream (oklch 0.94 0.01 90) for warmth, never pure white

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Diagonal section dividers cutting across the page
- Oversized type bleeding off-screen edges
- Content blocks staggered left/right with heavy negative space between
- Menu displayed as a typographic editorial spread

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick horizontal rules in amber as section dividers
- Large italic serif numerals as decorative elements
- Grain/noise texture overlay at 8% opacity across all sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states reveal content with a "curtain pull" effect
- Scroll-triggered text reveals using clip-path animations
- Menu items expand in place with a smooth height transition

**Animation:**
- Entrance: text slides in from bottom with staggered delay
- Hover: underline draws from left to right
- Page transitions: fade with slight upward drift

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold italic) — dramatic, editorial
- Body: DM Sans (regular/medium) — clean, modern contrast
- Accent labels: DM Mono (uppercase, tracked) — technical precision
</text>

## Approach 2: Organic Warmth — Japanese-Mediterranean Fusion
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Wabi-Sabi Gastronomy

**Core Principles:**
- Warmth through natural materials: linen textures, bamboo tones, deep forest greens
- Breathing space — generous whitespace that lets food photography speak
- Layered depth through subtle shadows and translucent overlays
- Handcrafted feel with slightly irregular, organic shapes

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm linen (oklch 0.97 0.015 85) — like aged paper
- Primary: deep forest green (oklch 0.35 0.09 155) — the bamboo and greenery of the interior
- Accent: warm gold (oklch 0.78 0.13 75) — candlelight and brass fixtures
- Dark: deep espresso (oklch 0.22 0.02 45) — rich, grounded

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric two-column layouts with one dominant image and offset text
- Hero section: full-bleed image with text anchored to bottom-left
- Menu as a grid of cards with generous padding and subtle hover elevation
- Sections separated by thin organic lines, not hard dividers

**Signature Elements:**
- Circular crop frames for food photography
- Thin gold border accents on key cards and headings
- Subtle linen/paper texture in background sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Soft, slow hover transitions (300ms ease)
- Image zoom-in on hover (scale 1.03)
- Reservation button pulses gently to draw attention

**Animation:**
- Fade-in with slight upward drift on scroll
- Staggered card reveals in menu section
- Parallax on hero image (subtle, 15% depth)

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (light italic, large) — refined, organic
- Body: Nunito Sans (regular 400) — approachable, clean
- Labels: Cormorant Garamond (small caps, spaced) — editorial elegance
</text>

## Approach 3: Dark Luxury — Cinematic Fine Dining
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury / Cinematic Gastronomy

**Core Principles:**
- Deep, moody backgrounds that make food photography glow
- Gold and copper metallics as the sole accent palette
- Cinematic proportions: wide-format sections, full-bleed imagery
- Restrained elegance — every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: deep near-black with warm undertone (oklch 0.14 0.015 45) — like a candlelit room
- Primary accent: burnished gold (oklch 0.76 0.16 75) — luxury, warmth
- Secondary: muted copper (oklch 0.62 0.12 50) — depth and richness
- Text: warm cream (oklch 0.93 0.015 85) — never cold white

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-viewport hero with centered text and a thin gold horizontal rule
- Alternating full-bleed and contained sections for rhythm
- Menu displayed as a dark card grid with gold borders
- Footer: rich dark with gold dividers and social links

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal rules as section separators
- Subtle vignette on all hero/banner images
- Metallic shimmer animation on the logo/brand name

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Gold underlines animate on hover
- Buttons have a subtle metallic gradient that shifts on hover
- Smooth scroll with section snap points

**Animation:**
- Hero text: letter-spacing expands on load (from tight to normal)
- Section reveals: fade in with 0.4s ease-out
- Menu cards: lift with box-shadow on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (regular + bold) — classic luxury
- Body: Lato (300 light) — clean, airy contrast
- Accent: Cinzel (uppercase, tracked) — Roman grandeur
</text>
